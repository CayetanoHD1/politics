Quick Downloads Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [7/9/2023]

This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is collected, used, and shared in the "Quick Downloads" application. By using our application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data Collection

  • Video URL Information: Quick Downloads allows users to enter URLs of videos from YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook for downloading. We do not store or collect these URLs or the downloaded content on our servers.
  • Technical Data: The application may collect technical data, such as device information and usage logs, to improve the user experience and resolve technical issues. This data is kept anonymous and is not linked to personal identities.

Use of Information

  • Video Downloads: The application uses the URLs provided by the user to download videos from the mentioned platforms. We do not share or store these videos.
  • Application Improvement: The technical data collected is used to enhance the quality and performance of the application.

Information Sharing

  • Third Parties: Quick Downloads does not share personal information with third parties. User-provided video URLs are solely used for video downloads and are not shared with any external entities.

Data Security

We do not store user information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our data collection and usage practices. Significant changes will be notified through application updates.
